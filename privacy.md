# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cookie Baker's Haven! We're so glad you're here, ready to bake up some delicious cookies. At Cookie Baker's Haven, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We want you to feel comfortable and confident using our site, so please take a moment to read through this policy.

**What Information We Collect**

We collect information in a few different ways:

*   **Information You Provide Directly:** When you sign up for our newsletter, create an account, purchase a product, or contact us, we may collect information like your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. Don't worry, we only collect what's necessary to provide you with the best possible experience!

*   **Automatically Collected Information:** When you visit Cookie Baker's Haven, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and the dates/times of your visits. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect this information (more on that below!).

*   **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve your experience on Cookie Baker's Haven. We use cookies to remember your preferences, track your website usage, and personalize content. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our site.

**How We Use Your Information**

We use your information for the following purposes:

*   **To Provide and Improve Our Services:** We use your information to process your orders, deliver content, personalize your experience, and improve our website and services.
*   **To Communicate with You:** We may use your information to send you newsletters, updates, promotional offers, and respond to your inquiries. You can always opt-out of receiving marketing emails from us.
*   **To Protect Our Rights and Interests:** We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, protect our legal rights, and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

**Sharing Your Information**

We may share your information with the following types of third parties:

*   **Service Providers:** We work with trusted service providers who help us operate our website, process payments, deliver emails, and analyze data. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
*   **Business Partners:** We may partner with other businesses to offer you products or services. In these cases, we will only share your information with your explicit consent.
*   **Legal Authorities:** We may disclose your information to legal authorities if required by law or legal process.

**Your Rights**

You have the right to:

*   **Access your personal information.**
*   **Correct inaccurate personal information.**
*   **Delete your personal information.**

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
